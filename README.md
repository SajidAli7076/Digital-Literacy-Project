## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course to develop essential skills required in today’s digital world. It focuses on understanding digital tools, building a professional online presence, improving communication skills, and spreading awareness about cybersecurity. Through this project, I explored various platforms and gained hands-on experience in using them effectively for academic and professional purposes. The project also helped me understand responsible digital behavior and the importance of staying safe online.

---

## 🧩 Detailed Tasks Description

### 🔹 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a visually appealing infographic using Canva. The infographic explains digital literacy and its importance for students. It covers safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence. This task helped me improve my design and presentation skills and understand how visual communication can be used effectively.

---

### 🔹 Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build my digital portfolio. GitHub is used to showcase projects, LinkedIn helps in professional networking, and Kaggle supports skill development. This task helped me understand the importance of building a strong online presence for future career opportunities.

---

### 🔹 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank, which helped improve my problem-solving skills. I also created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms with both multiple-choice and short-answer questions. The responses are stored in Google Sheets for analysis. This task improved my technical and analytical skills.

---

### 🔹 Task 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
In this task, I learned how to write professional emails. I drafted emails with proper subject lines, greetings, and structure. I also created a checklist for responsible social media usage. This task helped improve my communication skills and understanding of professional etiquette.

---

### 🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I studied cybercrimes such as phishing and online fraud. I created a case study explaining how these crimes happen and prepared a prevention checklist. This task increased my awareness about cybersecurity and safe online practices.
