# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Use a professional and respectful tone in all posts and comments  
- Think before posting and consider the long-term impact on your reputation  
- Keep your privacy settings updated and protect your personal information  
- Share accurate, reliable, and verified information only  
- Build a positive online presence by sharing achievements, skills, and academic work  

## Don’ts
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Avoid sharing excessive personal or sensitive information online  
- Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or negative discussions  
- Avoid spreading fake news, rumors, or unverified content  
- Do not post anything that could harm your reputation or future career opportunities  

## Conclusion
Responsible use of social media is essential for college students, as online behavior directly affects personal and professional life. Maintaining a positive digital presence, being mindful of what is shared, and avoiding harmful or inappropriate content can help build credibility and trust. By following these simple do’s and don’ts, students can use social media safely, effectively, and responsibly.
