# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Use a professional and respectful tone in posts and comments
- Think before posting and consider the long-term impact
- Keep your privacy settings updated and protect personal information
- Share accurate and verified information
- Build a positive online presence by sharing achievements, skills, and projects

## Don’ts
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
- Avoid sharing too much personal or sensitive information
- Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified content
- Do not post anything that could harm your reputation or future opportunities
